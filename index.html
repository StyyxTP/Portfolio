About me

Hello there ! I'm Maxime Mimault, I'm a new web developper.

My journey in development began during my high school years when I took Digital Science and Computer Science (NSI soit Numérique et Science de l'Informatique en français) courses, where I first encountered Python.

However, after completing my high school education, I chose to pursue a program in audiovisual studies with a focus on editing, as it greatly interested me at the time. After two years in this field, I came to realize that it didn't align with my true passion. That's when I made the decision to return to development, opting for web development to rekindle my passion for it.
